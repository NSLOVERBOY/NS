1BHK

The term "1BHK" or "one-bedroom house" refers to a home with just one bedroom, one hall, one kitchen, one bathroom, and one dining/living room, which together take up between 450 and 600 square feet of carpet space.

2BHK

A 2BHK apartment typically has one master bedroom and one smaller bedroom, along with a living room, kitchen, and dining area. It may also have a small workspace or extra storage.

3BHK

A 3BHK flat is usually between 1,200 and 1,800 square feet.
This size can provide space for a kitchen, dining area, living area, three bedrooms, and two or more bathrooms.
